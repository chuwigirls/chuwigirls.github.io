Enter the Nature

Naras Nature is playable by authorizing your Discord account via OAuth2 . This process is the same as authorizing a Discord bot access to your Discord account. The only information we retain is your Discord User ID and username, purely for verification purposes in the Naras Nature ARPG.

Please note: You may be redirected to a screen that says Discord App Launched. You can click on 'Continue to Discord' or wait a few seconds to be redirected to a screen that says 'Narrie wants to access your Discord account.' Then, you can authorize your Discord account.
