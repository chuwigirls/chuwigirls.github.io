Learn about Magic and train your Nara

Magic is a completely optional ARPG aspect of Naras Nature. Participation grants extra rewards, but you are not missing out on Naras Nature if you do not join.

Once a week, you can train each of your Naras to level up their Magic class.

Your Nara can train in one of the classes below:

  • Elemental
  • Divination
  • Taming
  • Magicraft
  • Alchemy
  • Deep arts

Elemental

Elemental magic draws from the surroundings, taking form from the energies that fill the Nature. These basic energies include the elements that make up an ecosystem, such as fire, water, wind, and earth, or more complex compounds like ice, lightning, or gravity.

Divination

Divine magic is granted by others; instead of drawing from oneself or the surroundings. Higher beings and authorities. In basic forms, divine magic can be used for healing and foretelling.
