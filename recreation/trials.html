Trials

Read more

Trials are a combination of prompts and questlines to complete for rewards or personal character development. Most Trials are completely optional; you do not have to complete Trials, except Narlings evolution Trials, to participate in Naras Nature. Rewards range from crystals, Palcharms, emblems, features, and more!

Why should you do a Trial? All Trials award additional crystals on top of regular crystal Currency costs. For example, if you draw a fullbody for a Trial, it will net you the regular 25 on top of any additional rewards given out by that Trial. Why not draw your Nara in a Trial and get that sweet extra crystals?

Emblems are cosmetic badges to embellish your Nara's masterlist. Some can award additional Crystals when the Nara it is equipped to is used in other Trials or recreational activities. When depicting multiple Naras in an Emblem submission, only the Naras that are the main subjects and follow that Trial's requirements will be rewarded the Emblem.

Each Trial will have its own requirements. Some must be done in consecutive order, may be done more than once, require another Nara or need a specific feature. Depiction requirements are low to encourage creativity in your Trials; perhaps it would be better to show your Nara's head peeking out the water for their swimming hobby, or a halfbody shot of your Nara's legs and weapon standing over a monster corpse for a Hunting emblem, rather than force a fullbody or long word count. If you have any questions about a Trial, please ask in the Discord server's help desk or the commenting on the Toyhouse world.

Tag breakdown:

  • Prompt Trials have simple themes meant to inspire you on how to depict your Nara. These Trials are usually standalone and do not have entry requirements. Some may only occur for a period of time, like quick flash prompts or recurring holiday events.
  • Questline Trials are groups of Trials that can be done to evolve or enlighten your Nara. These Trials are meant to build upon your Nara's lore and for character development. Like a chain of quests, these Trials are related and may build upon each other, requiring greater effort but reaping greater rewards. You may only receive rewards once all of the Trials in a questline have been completed, unlike one-time entry prompt Trials.
  • Evolution Trials must be completed for Narlings to evolve and to use them in all aspects of Naras Nature, including other Trials. Full-fledged Naras can do these Trials at any time as well. Any Naras depicted in an approved Narling form in these Trials can earn an additional 5 Crystals. (EXPAND MORE)
  • Eternal Trials have no end date and can be done at any time.
  • Ephemeral Trials are small Trials that pop up and appear only for a short time. They may not return, or make rare, unplanned comebacks.
  • Seasonal Trials occur during certain parts of the year, or occur on a schedule and have planned comebacks.
  • Trials with the Feature or Emblem tags grants those rewards besides Crystals.
