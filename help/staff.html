The Crew at Naras Nature

Celestials are the staff at Naras Nature, making sure the community runs smoothly and Naras are handled in society.

Celestials are split up into executives and officers. Executives preside over the direction the group takes and has a higher stake, while

Officers contribute solely to processing work.

Creatives are those who are gifted with the ability to conjure Naras in the Nature, and thus must matchmake Naras to owners; in common terms, permanent and guest artists. They have licenses and are adept at utilizing Summoning Scrolls, and often are called to cast new Naras for events or in times of great need.

Civilians are the rest of the residents at Naras Nature. They make up the bulk of the Nature, and without them, there would be no functioning society. Thanks for being a Civilian in Naras Nature!
