1.0 Vocabulary

  • Mentions of "Naras Nature," "Celestial(s)," "we," "us," and "our" refer to the Naras Nature species group, Celestial staff and moderators, owners, and co-owners.
  • Mentions of "you," "Civilian(s)," "member," and "user" refers to yourself the community members of Naras Nature.
  • Mentions of "this group," "this service," "this species," and "this website" refers to the Naras Nature website and its associated domains, including: https://narasnature.netlify.com, the Naras Nature Discord server, Narrie staff account and bot account, the Toyhouse world and account, and the DeviantArt group.
  • Mentions of "tickets," "sending in a ticket," "creating a ticket," "report" or similar language refers to the process of using the Discord server's Ticket bot to privately contact the Naras Nature staff.
  • Mentions of Naras Nature "commodities" refers to art, designs, assets, characters, in-game currency and items within Naras Nature.

2.0 Third-Party Compliance

Naras Nature is playable only via joining the Discord server, being personally verified by the moderators, and then by authorizing your Discord account on this website. In this way, all users of Naras Nature must comply with Discord's terms.

2.1 Age Requirements

In compliance with Discord's terms, all Civilians of Naras Nature must be 13 years of age or older to access gameplay.

In compliance with Paypal's terms, Civilians who are residents of the United States must be 18 years or older to be able to open a U.S. PayPal account and use its services. Naras Nature does not allow underage users to advertise or process services using PayPal within the group.

2.2 Third-Party Use

In the alpha and beta testing of Naras Nature, Naras Nature characters and items cannot be advertised for sale, trade, or free outside the Natures Nature Discord server, Toyhouse world, or DeviantArt group. Any advertisements found on third-party sites will result in a warning.

Any use or mention of third parties within the group such as Toyhouse, DeviantArt, Paypal, Discord, Twitter, Instagram, Bluesky, etc. means the user must comply with that third party site's Terms of Service at all times. Failure to comply with third party Terms of Service may result in punishment similar to violating Naras Nature's terms.

As Naras Nature is a private species, users may not trade, sell, or gift Nara designs, art, in-game currencies and items to users without a valid Naras Nature account. On sites that allow for character and art commerce, such as but not limited to: Toyhouse, DeviantArt, or Discord servers, Naras Nature commodities cannot be advertised for sale without disclosing the fact that both users must have a valid Naras Nature account. If found violating this rule, this will result in a strike.

3.0 User Conduct & Content

Naras Nature is PG-13, and as such, conduct and content within the Naras Nature community must be befitting of that parental guideline. This is to include users of all ages 13 and above, promoting a safe but open space. If you are unsure if you should mention something or if your submission's content are allowed, feel free to send in a ticket or ask the Celestials for more information.

3.1 Content not allowed in Naras Nature

  • Heavy swearing. Light swearing within the Discord is allowed.
  • Reclaimed slurs. As staff cannot verify the personal identities of users, we ask for users to refrain from using slurs, reclaimed or not.
  • NSFW topics or art, which includes heavy gore, violence, sexual content, nudity, or other graphic depictions.
  • Promoting for or mentions of hard drugs or substance abuse. Mentions of alcohol or recreational drug use are fine in art/writing depicitons or in passing conversation.
  • Mentioned in passing conservation does not allow for starting or diverting conservation into explicit alcohol or drug use; examples:
  • Allowed: "We were drinking at my birthday party and I made the dumbest decision." This conversation will be about the dumb decision, which may have been caused by inebriation, but does not explicitly go on about alcohol use.
  • Allowed: "I had a weird experience with my co-worker. I think they were high, but what they said was crazy." This passing mention is a possible excuse for this co-worker's actions, but should not focus on their affected state.
  • Not allowed: Explicit mentions of brands or types, routine mentions of substance use, introducing substances, or creating an exclusive culture surrounding substances with minors present.
  • Religion, politics, or controversal topics.
  • If a user politely asks to move on from the topic, the users participating in the controversial topics must move on. If users are deemed to be oppressing non-controversial topics, please send in a ticket to the Naras Nature staff. In most cases, if users are uncomfortable with a topic, we ask to move the topic outside of Naras Nature.
  • Spam, such as copypastas, large walls of text, 'fancy' text, ghost pinging, etc.
  • Any sort of speech, depiction, or bigortry that promotes hate or oppression, such as but not limited to racism, transphobia, homophobia, or sexism.

3.2 Community Guidelines

On top of disallowed content, users of Naras Nature are expected to follow the following community guidelines. By following this code of conduct, we aim to promote an inclusive and fair space for all users.

  • Any drama or gossip about users in the group or others outside Naras Nature is discouraged. If you have an issue with a user in the group or bring attention to an issue, please send in a ticket for review.
  • Arguments or disagreements between users should be reported to staff so that we may resolve it. If staff is not able to handle the conversation ASAP, users may ask the offending users to move their conversation outside the server. Otherwise, please let Naras Nature Celestials handle any disagreements.
  • Voicing displeasure or passive-agressive behavior to any user or staff is not allowed. If you suspect a user to be treating you or others in a disrespectful way, please screenshot all instances and create a ticket with staff. Repeated instances of disrespectful behavior will build a case, as Naras Nature respects those who may have different communication styles, and do not want to miscontrue such cases.
  • Cheating, foulplay, or exploiting loopholes is not allowed in any circumstance. We encourage users to report any suspected activity. If you encounter an issue or the site incorrectly doles out Naras Nature commodities, please report it.

Depending on the severity most violations will first result in a warning. In subsequent and repeated cases, a strike or suspension will be issued. Continued misconduct will result in a ban and termination of the user's account.

Any sort of hate speech will result in an immediate ban. Naras Nature is a safe space that will not tolerate bigotry.

Some content is allowed in the Discord server, but should be hidden with the Spoil toggle and appropriately labeled with a content warning (CW) accompanying the spoiled text/image. This content includes:

  • Common phobias; including by not limited to:
  • Insects and insectoid designs
  • Medical topics & designs, and medical instruments such as needles
  • Body horror, such as exposed organs or candy gore
  • Blood, weapons, or substances, including cartoonish depictions

4.0 Your Naras Nature Account

Naras Nature is playable by authorizing your Discord account via OAuth2 . This process is the same as authorizing a Discord bot to your account. Through this process, we expect your Discord account to comply with Discord's terms. Violating Discord's age requirements will result in a ban from the group associated with that Discord account.

Please note, as our site verifies users by their Discord roles, leaving the Discord server renders your account inaccessible. This is to ensure users comply with Naras Nature's content rating and community values.

4.1 Information Accessed and Retained

By authorizing your Discord account on the Naras Nature site, we retain minimal information about your Discord account for identification purposes.

The only information we retain on our site is your Discord User ID and username, purely for verification purposes in the Naras Nature ARPG. This information is publicly available to all Discord users.

The information we access is your Discord roles, to ensure you have been verified in the Discord server. We do not retain this information.

We do not access or retain any other information from your Discord account.

4.2 What you can do with your account

With a valid Naras Nature account, users may participate in any aspect of the group as allowed within game rules. Any of the actions list below may be done on any property of Naras Nature, such as the Discord server, Toyhouse world, DeviantArt, or between two users with valid Naras Nature accounts. These actions include:

  • Purchase, sell, own, trade, or create Naras Nature designs and characters.
  • Sell commissions offering designs for monetary gain or in-game currency and items.
  • Offer art or writing trades for designs or in-game currency and items.
  • Host user-led games, contests, or giveaways offering designs or in-game currency and items.
  • Participate in Naras Nature recreation, which includes dailies, pursuits, magic, magicomp, and other activities such as commerce.
  • Terminate your Naras Nature account. Read more at Account Freezing.

These actions are allowed for users provided the user's account does not have any outstanding strikes, suspensions, or bans.

4.3 What you are not allowed do with your account

All users in Naras Nature, under any circumstances, may not do any of the following:

  • Sell, trade, offer, gift, give away, or advertise Naras Nature designs, art, and in-game currencies and items to users without a valid Naras Nature account. Advertisements will result in a warning, while any business done will result in a strike, suspension of activities, or a ban depending on severity.
  • In the alpha and beta testing of Naras Nature, users may not do any of the above listed outside of Naras Nature properties, as Naras Nature is a private species not yet open to the public.
  • Create or use alternate accounts on the Naras Nature site.
  • Alternate Discord accounts are allowed within the Discord server. To protect against cheating with multiple accounts or accidental login on site, alternate Discord accounts that have joined the Discord server must be introduced as such during verification. That alternate account will be registered on site then marked as an alternate account to prevent useage or accidental login on the Naras Nature site.
  • Create new accounts to bypass bans or suspensions.
  • Violate the Terms of Service or any other laws and regulations that affect this website and its users, including third-party terms of service that are affiliated with used within Naras Nature.

4.4 Account freezing

Users may request to freeze their Naras Nature account at any time. By freezing your account, this means to hide your Naras Nature account from users on site. This does not permanently freeze or delete your account, and can be undone anytime by sending in a new ticket. Accounts are never deleted from site, but kept in the Naras Nature backend as an archive of all users that have accessed Naras Nature. Freezing your account does not affect your participation or membership in the Discord server. Account freezing must be requested within the Discord server by sending in a ticket.

By freezing your account, you cannot:

  • Participate in site activities.
  • Obtain, create, or remove Nara commodities from your account.

Frozen accounts are different from banned accounts in that frozen accounts can resume activity at any given time, if requested. Banned accounts cannot be unbanned nor allowed back in Naras Nature.
